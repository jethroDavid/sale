Frequently Asked Questions

Why did my product tracking fail?

Some websites have enhanced security measures or anti-bot systems that prevent SaleSavie from accessing their content. If your tracking fails after 3 attempts, try a different website or make sure the URL is correct.

 How accurate are the prices and availability?

SaleSavie works hard to get accurate information, but websites change their layouts frequently. If you notice any inaccuracies, please let us know by contacting support.

 How do I remove a tracked product?

Swipe left on any product card in either the Price Tracker or Availability Checker tab to reveal the delete button. Tap the trash icon to remove the product from your tracking list.

 What's the difference between price tracking and availability checking?

Price tracking monitors products that are already available but you want to buy at a lower price. Availability checking monitors out-of-stock products to notify you when they become available for purchase again.
