How To Use

Welcome to SaleSavie!

Your personal price tracker that helps you save money by notifying you when your favorite products go on sale.

Getting Started

Step 1: Add a product URL

Copy the URL of any product you want to track from online stores like Amazon, eBay, Walmart, etc.

Paste the URL in the input field on the home page and click "Track Price".

Tip: Make sure the URL leads directly to a product page, not a search results or category page.

Step 2: Monitor price changes

SaleSavie will check the price of your tracked products regularly (usually once per day).

You can see the price history in the chart when you click on a tracked product.

Tip: Use the date filters to view price changes within a specific time range.

Step 3: Get notified of price drops

When a price drop is detected, SaleSavie will automatically send you an email notification.

Click the link in the email to visit the product page and take advantage of the lower price.

Tip: Check your spam folder if you don't see notifications, and add our email to your contacts.

FAQ

Why did my product tracking fail?

Some websites have enhanced security measures or anti-bot systems that prevent SaleSavie from accessing their content. If your tracking fails after 3 attempts, try a different website or make sure the URL is correct.

 How accurate are the prices?

SaleSavie works hard to get accurate prices, but websites change their layouts frequently. If you notice any inaccuracies, please let us know by contacting support.

 How do I remove a tracked product?

Swipe left on any product card on the home page to reveal the delete button. Tap the trash icon to remove the product from your tracking list.

 Why should I donate?

SaleSavie is developed and maintained by a solo developer. Your donations help cover server costs, development time, and enable new features. Every contribution, no matter how small, is greatly appreciated!
